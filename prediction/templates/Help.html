The Main Purpose Of This Website Is To Give Farmers Basic Idea About What Price They Can Expect From Cotton Crop Before Cultivation/Sowing.
So That, If Predicted Price Is Less Than What They Are Expecting To Get After Harvesting, Then They Can Decide Whether To Go With
Production Of Cotton Crop Or Produce Some Other Crop Which Gives Better Exchange For Their Efforts.
